# Misalignment Between Backpropagation and the Hierarchy of Brain Responses to Images

**Date:** 2026-05-27  
**arXiv:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.28693

Backpropagated gradients in deep networks do not match the spatial and temporal hierarchy of the human brain, despite forward activations aligning well. This suggests fundamentally different learning mechanisms between artificial networks and biological systems.
